Streamlining the Workflow

Whether you are a patient or a diabetes provider, SweetSpot Therapeutics streamlines all device related initiation workflow for a seamless experience from prescribing a device to learning how to use the device and interpreting the data gathered from the device

Prescribing a device

We have partnered with device companies.
Prescriptions for devices are sent directly to the manufacturers headquarters via their integrated Provider Portal for immediate processing. We place emphasis on timely responses for approval/denial, shipping information if approved, and cost transparency. This reduces the time to initiation of a device while creating an environment of trust between the patient, their providers and the manufacturer. A device will only be initiated if the patient approves of all possible cost scenarios based on health plan and deductible.

Device Training

We have partnered with device companies.
There are multiple steps involved in the training process. From obtaining the product to proper training, our three step pre-training/training/post-training program simplifies the process, ensures proper training and adherence to therapy. The device company is made part of the care coordination team at the time of training. Device company representatives address all technical questions related to their product as long as the patient is wearing their product and monitor for signs of non-adherence.

Data review/interpretation (Remote patient monitoring)

We have partnered with device companies.
Data management platforms have come a long way in terms of data aggregation from various devices, yet there is no simple way to dictate the interpretation of that data directly into the EMR . Unfortunately, the report still has to be printed and then used for dictation of the data into the EMR. Our side-by-side review/interpretation page turns the review/interpretation process into an easy one step process so the practice remains paperless. The data obtained from all glucose sensors and insulin delivery devices is all managed on one page, at point of care and in between virtual visits for remote patient monitoring purposes.

Automated Billing

We automate billing for device training and remote patient monitoring

Device Training

A provider has to navigate the device company website to bill appropriately for device training. The SweetSpot Therapeutics billing platform automatically generates the proper CPT/ RPM code immediately following completion of the device training checklist.

Review/Interpretation of Glucose Data

At Point of Care: Billing codes must be entered manually into the EMR at point of care following review and interpretation by providers. Unfortunately that step is occasionally omitted. With SweetSpot Therapeutics, once the review/interpretation is completed, at point of care or in between visits, pressing the send button will not only save the form for audit, but will also generate proper billing.

Non Face to Face

We automate the monthly non-face-to-face billing for CGM review and interpretation so your practice remains paperless and captures that monthly income while you focus on diabetes outcomes and continue to provide a hand-holding approach to diabetes care. Without an automated billing process for review and interpretation that income is often not captured and billing unfortunately occurs randomly.
