The Problem

SweetSpot Therapeutics was built in response to the growing dissatisfaction among diabetes providers and people living with diabetes as the process of implementation of the numerous diabetes related technology tools added more strain on diabetes practices, negatively impacting the provider-patient experience and ultimately restricting the patient access to the practice.

Our Solution

As advocates for people living with diabetes and their providers, we developed SweetSpot Therapeutics with a few goals in mind: to improve access to diabetes care, to restore the provider-patient experience, and to address provider burnout, while focussing on diabetes outcomes leveraging all the available, sophisticated diabetes related technology.

Our Mission

Our mission is to alleviate the burden of diabetes. We tap into resources from our prestigious academic institutions such as Washington University and Saint Louis University among other universities along with well established endocrinology practices to introduce a Tele-Diabetes care model.

There is no single all encompassing digital diabetes platform for all people living with diabetes

In addition to the rising adoption of diabetes related technology including CGM, Insulin Delivery Devices, and the Artificial Pancreas among people living with diabetes, the management of both Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes is converging.

SGLT2 Inhibitors and GLP1-RA are turning out to be very effective for the treatment of Type 1 diabetes. Furthermore, a growing population of individuals with Type 2 diabetes is now on intensive insulin therapy.

The approach to Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes management shows some degree of convergence, yet there is no all encompassing digital diabetes platform for all people living with diabetes.

A Common Thread To The Entire Diabetes Ecosystem

The ideal diabetes playbook is one which addresses both the medical and technological needs.

Welcome to SweetSpot Therapeutics.

Our platform provides the infrastructure to accelerate the diabetes related digital journey for providers and people living with diabetes, and is positioning itself at the center of the entire diabetes ecosystem through its partnership network.
